The Academy

Curriculum

We're biased. We think coding is an awesome job. But not when you are using boring tools in a boring environment. We aim to directly transmit the things we think are cool about programming. And for us that means playing with open source software in terminals, hacking anything we dream up. We also really care about the company of others and being part of a community dedicated to doing cool stuff.

Our curriculum is a bit abnormal compared to your typical code school. We place a very large emphasis on development environment and mindset. We believe in teaching advanced concepts up front and trusting wizards to learn as they go. If nothing else, our goal is to teach you to take nothing seriously, while taking everything seriously, and realizing that you already know how to code.

The only requirement for joining the Academy is a willingness to put everything you think you know about programming aside and open your mind
